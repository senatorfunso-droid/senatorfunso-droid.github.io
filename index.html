About

Technology leadership built on faith, integrity, and results

Olufunso Festus Tokede is the CEO and Executive Director of Funshy Audio Visual Limited, a technology and audiovisual solutions company serving the hospitality and corporate sectors across Nigeria. He simultaneously holds the position of Senior IT Manager in the hospitality industry, bringing over a decade of hands-on experience to every engagement.

His technical depth spans network engineering, IT infrastructure, AV system integration, building automation systems, and digital guest experience technology. Beyond the technical work, he leads business strategy, team development, and client relationships at executive level.

Guided by a personal mission to build globally respected technology businesses that honour God and serve others with excellence, Olufunso brings both entrepreneurial drive and operational discipline to every project he takes on.
